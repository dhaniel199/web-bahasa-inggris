Simple Present Tense

Apa itu Simple Present Tense?

Simple Present Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kebiasaan, fakta umum, rutinitas, dan perasaan atau pendapat yang bersifat tetap.

Struktur Kalimat

  • Positif: Subject + Verb (add -s/-es for he, she, it)
    Contoh: She walks to school every day.
  • Negatif: Subject + do/does + not + Verb
    Contoh: They do not play football.
  • Pertanyaan: Do/Does + Subject + Verb?
    Contoh: Does he like coffee?

Contoh Kalimat

  • I drink tea every morning.
  • The sun rises in the east.
  • We do not eat meat.
  • Does she study English?

Latihan Pilihan Ganda

Pilih jawaban yang benar untuk melengkapi kalimat dalam Simple Present Tense:

1. She ___ to school every day.

2. They ___ (not/play) football on weekends.

3. ___ he ___ (like) to read books?

4. The sun ___ in the east.

5. We ___ (not/go) to the park every evening.

Past Tense

Apa itu Past Tense?

Past Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian atau aktivitas yang telah selesai di masa lalu.

Struktur Kalimat

  • Positif: Subject + Verb (past form, add -ed for regular verbs)
    Contoh: She walked to school yesterday.
  • Negatif: Subject + did + not + Verb (base form)
    Contoh: They did not play football.
  • Pertanyaan: Did + Subject + Verb (base form)?
    Contoh: Did he like coffee?

Contoh Kalimat

  • I visited my grandmother last weekend.
  • The sun set at 6 PM yesterday.
  • We did not watch the movie.
  • Did she study English?

Latihan Pilihan Ganda

Pilih jawaban yang benar untuk melengkapi kalimat dalam Past Tense:

1. She ___ to the market yesterday.

2. They ___ (not/visit) the museum last week.

3. ___ you ___ (see) the movie?

4. He ___ a book last night.

5. We ___ (not/eat) dinner at home yesterday.

Future Tense

Apa itu Future Tense?

Future Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan rencana, prediksi, atau kejadian yang akan terjadi di masa depan.

Struktur Kalimat

  • Positif: Subject + will + Verb (base form)
    Contoh: She will travel to Japan next month.
  • Negatif: Subject + will + not + Verb (base form)
    Contoh: They will not attend the party.
  • Pertanyaan: Will + Subject + Verb (base form)?
    Contoh: Will he call you?

Contoh Kalimat

  • I will visit my friends tomorrow.
  • It will rain this afternoon.
  • We will not go to the beach.
  • Will she join the club?

Latihan Pilihan Ganda

Pilih jawaban yang benar untuk melengkapi kalimat dalam Future Tense:

1. She ___ to Paris next week.

2. They ___ (not/attend) the meeting tomorrow.

3. ___ you ___ (call) me later?

4. It ___ tomorrow.

5. We ___ (not/stay) at home this weekend.

Present Continuous Tense

Apa itu Present Continuous Tense?

Present Continuous Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang sedang berlangsung saat ini atau sementara.

Struktur Kalimat

  • Positif: Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing
    Contoh: She is studying English now.
  • Negatif: Subject + am/is/are + not + Verb-ing
    Contoh: They are not playing football.
  • Pertanyaan: Am/Is/Are + Subject + Verb-ing?
    Contoh: Is he watching TV?

Contoh Kalimat

  • I am reading a book right now.
  • The children are playing in the park.
  • We are not working today.
  • Is she cooking dinner?

Latihan Pilihan Ganda

Pilih jawaban yang benar untuk melengkapi kalimat dalam Present Continuous Tense:

1. She ___ a book right now.

2. They ___ (not/play) in the garden.

3. ___ you ___ (watch) TV now?

4. He ___ dinner now.

5. We ___ (not/work) this afternoon.

Past Continuous Tense

Apa itu Past Continuous Tense?

Past Continuous Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang sedang berlangsung di masa lalu pada waktu tertentu.

Struktur Kalimat

  • Positif: Subject + was/were + Verb-ing
    Contoh: She was studying English at 7 PM yesterday.
  • Negatif: Subject + was/were + not + Verb-ing
    Contoh: They were not playing football.
  • Pertanyaan: Was/Were + Subject + Verb-ing?
    Contoh: Was he watching TV?

Contoh Kalimat

  • I was reading a book at 8 PM last night.
  • The children were playing in the park yesterday.
  • We were not working at that time.
  • Was she cooking dinner?

Latihan Pilihan Ganda

Pilih jawaban yang benar untuk melengkapi kalimat dalam Past Continuous Tense:

2. They ___ (not/play) in the yard yesterday.

3. ___ you ___ (watch) TV at 6 PM?

4. He ___ dinner at that time.

5. We ___ (not/work) at 8 PM yesterday.

Present Perfect Tense

Apa itu Present Perfect Tense?

Present Perfect Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan aksi yang telah selesai di masa sekarang atau memiliki dampak hingga saat ini.

Struktur Kalimat

  • Positif: Subject + have/has + Verb3
    Contoh: She has finished her homework.
  • Negatif: Subject + have/has + not + Verb3
    Contoh: They have not seen the movie.
  • Pertanyaan: Have/Has + Subject + Verb3?
    Contoh: Has he visited Paris?

Contoh Kalimat

  • I have just eaten lunch.
  • She has lived here for five years.
  • We have not finished the project.
  • Have they traveled abroad?

Latihan Pilihan Ganda

Pilih jawaban yang benar untuk melengkapi kalimat dalam Present Perfect Tense:

1. She ___ her homework.

2. They ___ (not/see) the new movie.

3. ___ you ___ (visit) London?

4. He ___ three books this year.

5. We ___ (not/travel) this summer.

Past Perfect Tense

Apa itu Past Perfect Tense?

Past Perfect Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan aksi yang selesai sebelum aksi lain di masa lalu.

Struktur Kalimat

  • Positif: Subject + had + Verb3
    Contoh: She had finished her work before the meeting.
  • Negatif: Subject + had + not + Verb3
    Contoh: They had not eaten dinner.
  • Pertanyaan: Had + Subject + Verb3?
    Contoh: Had he left already?

Contoh Kalimat

  • I had completed the task before noon.
  • She had gone home before it rained.
  • We had not seen the film.
  • Had they finished the report?

Latihan Pilihan Ganda

Pilih jawaban yang benar untuk melengkapi kalimat dalam Past Perfect Tense:

1. She ___ her work before the meeting.

2. They ___ (not/eat) before the party.

3. ___ you ___ (leave) before the event?

4. He ___ the book before class.

5. We ___ (not/see) the show before yesterday.
